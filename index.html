WHO WE ARE

"We are guided and inspired by our vision"

OUR MISSION

Partner, advise and train African Organisations on business development in various sectors
and help them in giving back to the society, to impact digital literacy on the African youth

OUR VISION

Bridge the digital literacy gap among African Organisations and youths in Sub – Saharan Africa
while promoting business development of African Organisations in various sectors

WHERE TO FIND US

Nairobi

Based in Nairobi, Kenya Teamense Africa has a regional focus to serve the entire African Continent, and promote
the growth and development of ICT and innovation in the Sub-saharan Africa by; empowering African Organisations and
the youth of Africa, while providing consultancy to help promote business development in Africa
